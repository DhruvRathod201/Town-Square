About TownSquare

Empowering citizens to build better communities through technology

Our Mission

TownSquare is dedicated to bridging the gap between citizens and local government, making civic engagement accessible, efficient, and impactful.

We believe that every voice matters and that technology can transform how communities address local issues. By providing a platform for citizens to report problems and track their resolution, we're building stronger, more responsive communities.

What We Do

Amplify Citizen Voices

Provide a platform for citizens to report issues that matter to them, ensuring their concerns are heard and addressed.

AI-Powered Efficiency

Use artificial intelligence to automatically categorize and prioritize complaints, helping officials respond faster and more effectively.

Track Progress

Enable real-time tracking of issue resolution, providing transparency and accountability in local government operations.

How It Started

TownSquare was born from a simple observation: too many community issues go unreported or unresolved because citizens don't know how to reach the right people or track their requests.

Our founders experienced this firsthand when trying to report a broken streetlight in their neighborhood. After multiple phone calls and emails with no response, they realized there had to be a better way.

Today, TownSquare serves communities across the country, helping thousands of citizens report and track issues while giving local officials the tools they need to serve their communities more effectively.

A simple idea that changed everything
